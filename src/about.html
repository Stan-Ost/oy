Our mission

We feel swimwear should be like dresses. In dresses there are a lot of different styles such as the little black dress, the cocktail dress, and the evening gown. However, we felt there were no such distinctions in style when it came to the swimwear. Today, bathing suits look like underwear. They have the same shape and style as the most unflattering functional underwear. Of course, there are those that are quite beautiful. However, there is something so strikingly similar between even the most attractive bathing suits and underwear. We wanted to create a swimwear that had a more creative and fashionable shape, and made with flattering fabrics. The desire was to create elegant swimsuits that matches a woman’s dignity. I inspired by the beauty of dresses, visualized women dressed as elegantly in bathing suits as they are in dresses. Every design captures a woman’s ethereal elegance. Thus our designs elevate the bathing suit so that it is visually elegant and no longer an extension of women’s underwear. We gave you our the coctail , the little black , and the evening swimsuits.
